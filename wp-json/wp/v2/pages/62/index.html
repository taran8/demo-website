Greetings from the Principal.<\/span><\/p>\n

Lady Shri Ram College for Women (LSR), a premier educational institution of the University of Delhi. LSR was established in 1956 by late Sir Shri Ram in memory of his wife. His commitment to the cause of education was not merely philanthropic but was integral to his idea of nation building. Today, LSR has evolved into a gateway to excellence. We not only prove ourselves in academics but also in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. A look at the many\u00a0<\/span>university rank holders<\/strong>\u00a0across departments as well as the number of awards won at national and international sports and cultural competitions<\/span>,\u00a0<\/strong>the numerous<\/span>\u00a0distinguished visitors\u00a0<\/strong><\/a>\u00a0as well as the multitude of\u00a0<\/span>societies<\/strong><\/a>\u00a0that college is buzzing with bears testimony to the rich tapestry that characterises LSR.<\/span><\/p>\n

Our record of excellence has been acknowledged repeatedly by assessments made by many independent and reputed\u00a0surveys<\/strong><\/a>. We have been consistently ranked as No. 1 college in India for Liberal Arts and Social Sciences. We are also proud that LSR scored an A+ grade with CGPA of 3.61 awarded by\u00a0NAAC<\/strong><\/a>. It is a matter of pride that NAAC have given us 100% marks for our infrastructure. LSR has climbed to the 2nd<\/sup>\u00a0position in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF<\/a>) India rankings. \u00a0<\/strong>Our students benefit from a robust\u00a0Placement Cell<\/strong><\/a>\u00a0that works with the individual students throughout the year. Our excellence is recognized by the MHRD that instituted an\u00a0Institution Innovation Council<\/strong><\/a>\u00a0at Lady Shri Ram College. We are one of\u00a0the very few non-technical institutions in the country to be granted this recognition.<\/p>\n

While our students become global citizens through our numerous\u00a0international exchange<\/strong><\/a>\u00a0programmes or by securing admission in the best universities in the world, we are equally focused on rooting them in their social contexts. The students participate in many\u00a0outreach programmes<\/strong><\/a>\u00a0that allows them to transform lives around them.\u00a0REACH<\/strong><\/a>, an acronym for Reaffirming Equity, Access, Capacity and Humanism, is an initiative that acts as the equal opportunity cell of the college. It provides an inclusive platform and enhances the strength and capabilities of students coming from diverse backgrounds.<\/p>\n

Dear Aspirant,<\/h3>\n

I welcome you on behalf of the Birla Institute of Management Technology and thank you for showing your interest in us.<\/p>\n

For years BIMTECH has been educating young men and women who entered its community and attained their transformation into responsible citizens and leaders of corporates. By joining BIMTECH, you are embarking on an education system that is meant to be transformative \u2013 academically, socially, and personally. \u201cExcellence and Values\u201d are our guiding principles which are reflected in every activity of the Institute. Our Handbook clarifies those values and standards and we expect you to honour them in your conduct as a student in the College.<\/p>\n

Our mission at BIMTECH is to provide a safe, secure environment in which every student will acquire the skills and knowledge necessary to become accomplished, productive members of the ever-changing, global community. Our faculty is a very dedicated group of individuals who continue to focus on providing the best possible instruction for our students.<\/p>\n
